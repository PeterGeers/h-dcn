## Secties

### Sectie 1: The Gathering

**Titel:** `The Gathering`

**Tekst:**

```
The 2027 Presidents' Meeting brings approved federation clubs together for a focused weekend to discuss important topics, review Federation activity, and have a great time! Over 70 active clubs are expected to join.
```

### Sectie 2: Timetable

**Titel:** `Timetable`

**Tekst:**

```
Thursday: Arrival & PM Registration.
Friday: Welcome Party (20:00).
Saturday: Presidents' Meeting (10:00-17:00) + Evening Party.
Sunday: Checkout (before 12:00).
```

### Sectie 3: Registration Process

**Titel:** `Registration Process`

**Tekst:**

```
1. Open the registration and log in or create an account.
2. Select your approved club.
3. Fill in delegate and guest details, save progress anytime.
4. Review and submit your registration for organizer approval.
```

### Sectie 4: FAQ

**Titel:** `Frequently Asked Questions`

**Tekst:**

```
Who can register? Only delegates from approved FH-DCE member clubs.
Can I save and continue later? Yes, your draft is saved automatically.
What information should I prepare? Club name, delegate names, guest names, and accommodation preferences.
Payment? Payment details will be communicated after registration approval.
```
