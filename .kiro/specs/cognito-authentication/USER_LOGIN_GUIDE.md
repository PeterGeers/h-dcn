### First Time Login

1. **Go to the H-DCN website** and click "Login"
2. **Click "Email Recovery"** or "Forgot Password"
3. **Enter your email address** (the same one you used before)
4. **Check your email** for a verification code
5. **Enter the verification code** on the website
6. **Set up your passkey** when prompted:
   - Use your fingerprint (Touch ID/Windows Hello)
   - Use face recognition (Face ID)
   - Use a security key (YubiKey, etc.)
   - Or use your device's PIN/pattern

### Future Logins

Once you've set up your passkey, logging in is super easy:

1. **Go to the H-DCN website** and click "Login"
2. **Use your passkey** - just touch your fingerprint or use face recognition
3. **You're in!** No email or password needed
